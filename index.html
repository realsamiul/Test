The Scale: 256M Docs/Year

An estimated 256 million critical documents in Banking (66M), Healthcare (180M), and Land (8.5M) require manual processing annually.

The Cost: 64M Wasted Hours

At a conservative 15 minutes per document, this translates to 64 million hours of skilled labor lost to data entry, costing over Tk 32,000 Crore.

The Risk: 10-15% Error Rate

Manual entry error rates hover between 10-15%. This isn't just an inefficiency; it's a direct driver of fraud, compliance failures, and customer disputes.

The "Smart Bangladesh" Mandate

The government's "Smart Bangladesh 2041" vision mandates 100% paperless services. This has created an urgent, top-down market. The question is no longer *if* this will be solved, but *who* will provide the enabling infrastructure.
