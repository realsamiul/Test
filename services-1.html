Bucket 2: The "Brain" – Our Bengali LLM Specialists
This is the layer competitors lack. All models are open-source, self-hosted, and $0 cost.
TituLLM-3B (Hishab)
SOTA Bengali Reasoning: Our primary "Brain" for parsing amount words ("পঞ্চাশ হাজার" → 50,000) and complex commonsense logic. SOTA on Bengali CSQA, OBQA benchmarks.
BanglaBERT (BUET CSE)
SOTA Bengali Validation: SOTA on the BLUB benchmark (77.09). We use its 95.8% NER accuracy for high-accuracy name matching ("মোঃ করিম" → "Md. Karim").
Qwen3-235B (Alibaba)
SOTA Fraud Detection: Our most powerful reasoning engine, with 91% accuracy on fraud detection benchmarks. Used for final, cross-document validation. License: Free API.
Llama 3.1-8B (Meta)
SOTA Translation: Used for high-quality, 128K context Bengali-to-English translation for API outputs and reporting. License: Free.
