Handwritten Chaos
Challenge: Cursive Bengali "পঞ্চাশ হাজার", correction (60k→50k).

Cheque #2: BRAC Bank
Printed, High-Value
Challenge: Printed, but high-value (Tk 1.23 Crore) and uses "Crore" / "Lakh".
M0NARQ Result (1.9s): Routed to Qwen2.5-VL ($0 cost). Correctly parsed "One Crore..." → 12,345,678. Validated Figures=Words. Status: ✅ APPROVED (Flagged High-Value).
Cheque #3: Islami Bank
Complex Name
Challenge: Payee "Mohammad Shamsudin Akanda". Needs DB validation.
M0NARQ Result (2.1s): Routed to Qwen2.5-VL. BanglaBERT generated variants ("Md. Shamsudin," "M.S. Akanda") and matched payee to account holder. Status: ✅ APPROVED (Account Validated).
Cheque #4: Al-Arafah
Correction Mayhem
Challenge: Multiple corrections (Payee + Amount), faded ink, rotated scan.
M0NARQ Result (5.3s): Pre-processed (deskewed, enhanced). Routed to Claude 3.5. Detected 2 *initialed* corrections. Status: ⚠️ FLAGGED FOR REVIEW (Policy: >1 correction needs human sign-off).
