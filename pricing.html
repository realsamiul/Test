FAQ
01
How does M0NARQ achieve such low costs?
Our "Two-Bucket" architecture is the key. By using free, open-source models (Qwen, Nemotron, Docling) for 70-80% of documents and routing only the most challenging cases to paid APIs (Claude 3.5), we achieve near-zero marginal costs while maintaining superior accuracy. This intelligent routing is our economic moat.
02
What is your path to profitability?
We're already profitable in Year 1 with a 61% net margin. This is based on conservative, achievable targets: securing 3 bank clients by March 2026 and winning the UNDP Land Records pilot (Tk 47.5 Cr contract) by August 2026. Our capital efficiency (Tk 65L pre-seed) and near-zero marginal costs after initial setup create an exceptionally strong unit economics profile.
03
How do you compare to AWS and Google pricing?
We're 3.3x cheaper than AWS/GCP at $0.0006 per document vs. their $0.002. But the true cost savings are 10-15x when you factor in our ability to prevent fraud losses (Tk 1.23 Crore on a single fraudulent cheque) and our compliance with Bangladesh data sovereignty requirements that would otherwise require expensive custom infrastructure.
04
What is your customer acquisition strategy?
We're leveraging a top-down, government-driven adoption model. The "Smart Bangladesh 2041" vision mandates 100% paperless services, creating an urgent, top-down market. We're targeting the Land Records digitization project (UNDP pilot) as our marquee case study, then expanding to banking and healthcare using the credibility and technical proof from that implementation.
